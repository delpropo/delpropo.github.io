Background and Expertise

Structural Biology | X-ray Crystallography | Drug Discovery

Professional Summary

Jim DelProposto is a structural biologist with extensive expertise in X-ray crystallography, protein structure determination, and structure-based drug discovery. His work focuses on supporting medicinal chemistry programs through high-resolution structural analysis of protein-ligand complexes, enabling rational drug design and optimization.

Core Expertise

X-ray Crystallography

Expert in protein crystallization, X-ray diffraction data collection, structure determination, and refinement. Specialized in obtaining high-quality crystal structures of protein-ligand complexes for drug discovery applications.

Structural Biology

Extensive experience in macromolecular structure determination, including protein expression, purification, and characterization. Skilled in solving challenging structures and resolving complex binding modes.

Structure-Based Drug Design

Collaborates with medicinal chemistry teams to support drug discovery programs. Provides structural insights for lead optimization, including analysis of binding interactions and structure-activity relationships.

Protein Engineering

Experience with protein construct design, solubility enhancement, and crystallization optimization. Developed fusion tag methodologies to improve protein expression and crystallization success rates.

High-Throughput Crystallography

Proficient in automated crystallization screening, synchrotron data collection, and high-throughput structure determination pipelines to support multiple concurrent drug discovery projects.

Data Analysis & Visualization

Expert in crystallographic software suites, structure validation, and molecular graphics. Skilled in communicating structural findings to multidisciplinary teams.

Research Areas

  • PROTAC Development: Structural characterization of targeted protein degradation complexes
  • Protein-Protein Interactions: Structure-based discovery of inhibitors targeting PPIs (e.g., Menin-MLL)
  • Oncology Drug Discovery: Support for cancer therapeutic programs including androgen receptor and estrogen receptor degraders
  • Serine Protease Inhibitors: Structure determination for complement factor D and related targets
  • Methodological Development: Innovation in crystallization techniques and protein engineering approaches

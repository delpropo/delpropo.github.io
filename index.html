Jim DelProposto, Ph.D.

Structural Biologist | X-ray Crystallography Expert | Drug Discovery Specialist

Professional Overview

Dr. Jim DelProposto is an accomplished structural biologist with extensive expertise in X-ray crystallography and structure-based drug discovery. His research focuses on determining high-resolution protein structures to support medicinal chemistry programs, with particular emphasis on protein-ligand complexes, targeted protein degradation (PROTACs), and protein-protein interaction inhibitors.

With contributions to numerous high-impact publications in the Journal of Medicinal Chemistry and other leading journals, Dr. DelProposto has been instrumental in advancing drug discovery efforts in oncology and other therapeutic areas. His structural insights have enabled the rational design and optimization of novel therapeutics.

Areas of Expertise

X-ray Crystallography

Expert in protein crystallization, structure determination, and high-resolution analysis of protein-ligand complexes for drug discovery applications.

Structure-Based Drug Design

Provides critical structural insights for medicinal chemistry programs, enabling rational drug design and lead optimization strategies.

PROTAC Development

Specialized in structural characterization of targeted protein degradation complexes for next-generation therapeutics.

Protein Engineering

Developed innovative approaches for protein expression, purification, and crystallization optimization.

Research Contributions

Dr. DelProposto has contributed to breakthrough research in multiple therapeutic areas:

  • Cancer Therapeutics: Structural studies supporting development of androgen receptor and estrogen receptor degraders
  • Protein-Protein Interactions: Structure-based discovery of Menin-MLL inhibitors
  • Complement Inhibition: High-potency Factor D inhibitor development
  • Methodological Innovation: Development of novel crystallization and protein engineering techniques
